Design Concept

We wish our NFTs can represent more value than just a JPEG or a pass, we want it to represent the equality of blockchain. In blockchain, no matter your nation, culture, or skin colors, we are all the same here.

The MaVerse NFT design is based on four mythological creatures, Azure Dragon, White Tiger, Vermillion Bird, and Black Tortoise. There will be a total of 4 different collections and each NFT will have 7 traits with over 100 elements, making sure that each NFT is unique to others.

Ownership Benefit

DAO-Fi is a structure we developed for our holders, DAO represent to their power of making decision and Fi stands for the dividend they will receive. DAO-Fi members will have access to the holder club, free merchandise, dividend from game revenue and we will also reveal all our cashflow to DAO-Fi member. We are doing something never be done before and we believe we will become standard of NFT industry. Join Discord for more information or take a look of whitepaper.(超連結到白皮書, 網站到時候發給你)
